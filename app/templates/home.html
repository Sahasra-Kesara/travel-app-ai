    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our travel planning service

    What can I search here?

    You can search for tourist destinations, hotels, travel routes, tour guides, vehicles for transport, hospitals, and other essential travel services across Sri Lanka. Ask in any natural language - our AI understands your travel needs.

    Do I need exact place names?

    No! You can type natural questions like "best budget hotels in Kandy", "things to do around Galle", or "cheapest way to reach Ella". Our AI will understand and find relevant results for you.

    Can I search for travel routes?

    Absolutely! You can ask for routes between any two locations. Example: "Best way to travel from Colombo to Ella", "Train options from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya", or "How to reach Arugam Bay from Colombo". We provide multiple route options with details.

    Can I find emergency services?

    Yes! You can search for hospitals and medical facilities near any location. Example: "Hospitals near Sigiriya", "Medical services in Nuwara Eliya", or "Emergency clinics in Colombo". We help you locate the nearest healthcare facilities quickly.

    How accurate are the recommendations?

    Our AI uses comprehensive knowledge bases of verified destinations, hotels, routes, and services across Sri Lanka. Recommendations are based on real data and are regularly updated. Always verify current prices and availability before booking.
